Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024

Introduction

Welcome to the Malpeli App privacy policy. This policy explains how our app handles your data and permissions, with a particular focus on camera usage and other device features.

WebView Implementation

Our app primarily functions as a WebView wrapper for malpeli.co.uk. This means the app provides a mobile-optimized way to access our website's content and features.

Camera Permission Usage

The app requests camera permission for the following specific purposes:

  • QR Code Scanning: To scan QR codes when this feature is accessed through the website
  • Photo/Video Capture: To allow you to take photos or videos when uploading content through the website
  • File Upload: To enable camera access when choosing files to upload through the website
  • Flashlight/Torch: To provide flashlight functionality when needed

Important Note: The app itself does not store, collect, or transmit any photos, videos, or camera data. All media handling is done through the website's secure interface.

Data Collection and Storage

The app itself:

  • Does not store any photos or videos captured using the camera
  • Does not collect any camera or media data
  • Does not share camera access with third parties
  • Only provides camera access when explicitly requested by website features

Any data collection, storage, or processing is handled entirely by the website (malpeli.co.uk) according to its own privacy policy.

User Control

You maintain full control over camera permissions:

  • Camera permission can be granted or revoked at any time through your device settings
  • The app will request permission before accessing the camera for any feature
  • You can deny camera access and still use the app's other features
